Abu Zahid Jakaria Partho

Abu Zahid Jakaria Partho is a Bangladeshi freelance model, actor & also a Instrumental Music Composer who appears in music industry in 2018. Partho started his career by modeling. Actor & Music composer Abu Zahid Jakaria Partho, who gained attention for his good works. In 2020, he released his 1st hip-hop instrumental track "The Matha Nosto Beats". He made this track with some music directors of Bangladesh. After released his first single track 'The Matha Nosto Beats' an energetic and hip-hop track that launches his new career as a singer/music composer. Since then he has been working and collaborating with several artists and band groups.
